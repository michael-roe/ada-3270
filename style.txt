
--  Style Guide
--
--  Indentation is 3 characters per level for if, loop, and case.
--
--  An initialization or default expression on the line after a
--  declaration is indented by two spaces.
--
--  Reserved words are in lower case.
--
--  Identifiers start with an upper case letter.
--
--  Underscores separate words in an identifier.
--
--  In entity declarations, colons are surrounded by spaces.
--
--  Program units and subprograms have their name at the end.
--
